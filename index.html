About Us

We are a family-run, luxury ice-cream parlour based in the heart of Mullingar. For the last 6 years, we have served to our customers the very best sweet treats and hot beverages that we could provide. If you're visiting Mullingar, drop in for a chat about our local amenities.

Our Ingredients

The milk and butter used in our delicious desserts comes exclusively from a local, small-herd dairy. Run by the same family for over thirty years, the Marshall family dedicate their time to ensuring a happy and healthy life for their cows. Hens that run free over the 55 acre farm also share their eggs with us to make our irresistable cakes and cookies.

Here or To Go

We've got ice-cream every which way at SCOOPS! In a cone, tub, topped with extra treats or melting slowly over some warm chocolate brownies. YOU choose. Relax in our cosy parlour, take in the activity of our town at our outdoor, bistro tables or grab it to go with our eco-friendly, takeaway packaging. Just fancy a coffee? Enjoy our Fair-trade, fresh roasted beans and pick up a loyalty card for extra value.

Community

Here at SCOOPS we believe in being active in our town. Our crew are people who care the same way about our values of maintaining an earth-friendly business whilst supporting local produces and causes. We give a percentage of our profits every year to local 'Green' projects that will benefit future generations.
