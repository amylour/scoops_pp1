About Us

We are a family-run, luxury ice-cream parlour based in the heart of Mullingar. For the last 6 years, we have served to our customers the very best sweet treats and hot beverages that we could provide. If you're visiting Mullingar, drop in for a chat about our local amenities.

Our Ingredients

The milk and butter used in our delicious desserts comes exclusively from a local, small-herd dairy. Run by the same family for over thirty years, the Marshall family dedicate their time to ensuring a happy and healthy life for their cows.

Here or To Go

We've got ice-cream every which way at SCOOPS! In a cone, tub, topped with extra treats or melting slowly over some warm chocolate brownies. YOU choose. Relax in our cosy parlour or grab it to go with our eco-friendly, takeaway packaging. Just fancy a coffee? Enjoy our Fair-trade, fresh roasted beans.
